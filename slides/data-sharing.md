## Why can't hospitals just share data?

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-5 mt-4">

<div class="step-card" style="background: rgba(255,107,107,0.07); border-color: rgba(255,107,107,0.22);">
  <div style="font-family:'Roboto Mono'; color:#ff6b6b; font-weight:600; font-size:1.05em;">Regulation</div>
  <div style="font-size:0.85em; color:#c8b8a8; margin-top:0.3em;">GDPR, HIPAA and ethics boards forbid moving patient records across institutions or borders.</div>
</div>

<div class="step-card" style="background: rgba(255,107,107,0.07); border-color: rgba(255,107,107,0.22);">
  <div style="font-family:'Roboto Mono'; color:#ff6b6b; font-weight:600; font-size:1.05em;">Trust &amp; control</div>
  <div style="font-size:0.85em; color:#c8b8a8; margin-top:0.3em;">Institutions won't hand sensitive data to a third party &mdash; the legal and reputational risk stays with them.</div>
</div>

<div class="step-card" style="background: rgba(255,107,107,0.07); border-color: rgba(255,107,107,0.22);">
  <div style="font-family:'Roboto Mono'; color:#ff6b6b; font-weight:600; font-size:1.05em;">Consent</div>
  <div style="font-size:0.85em; color:#c8b8a8; margin-top:0.3em;">Patients agreed to a specific local use of their data, not to its redistribution.</div>
</div>

<div class="step-card" style="background: rgba(255,107,107,0.07); border-color: rgba(255,107,107,0.22);">
  <div style="font-family:'Roboto Mono'; color:#ff6b6b; font-weight:600; font-size:1.05em;">Security</div>
  <div style="font-size:0.85em; color:#c8b8a8; margin-top:0.3em;">Every copy and every transfer opens a new path for a breach.</div>
</div>

</div>
